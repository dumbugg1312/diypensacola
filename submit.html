booking a diy or punk show in pensacola? send us the flyer and we'll put it on the calendar, free, no gatekeeping. here's what to include:

  • the date
  • the venue (+ address if it's a house or DIY space)
  • set time / doors
  • cover: free, sliding scale, or a $ amount
  • all ages, 18+, or 21+
  • who's playing (the full lineup)
  • the flyer image
  • is it a benefit? who or what it's for
