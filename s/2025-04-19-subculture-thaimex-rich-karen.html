Thaimex, Rich Karen, Trundle Brothers, Quintessence, Cole Trickle

free

This Bicycle Day, April 19th, we at Subculture would like to extend a warm invitation for a peculiar kind of celebration as we honor the strange and wonderful world Dr. Albert Hoffman left behind after his world changing adventure on his bicycle one glorious spring day in 1934. We'll be kicking the celebration off with open decks from 7pm to 9pm followed by exquisite vibes from @richkarenmusic @quintessence_dnb and @dj_thaimex while also introducing The Trundle Brothers. Topping off the night we have @realcoletrickle providing a special 420 set at midnight. Extra subs. Extra love.
