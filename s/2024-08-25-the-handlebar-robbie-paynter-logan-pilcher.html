The Handlebar · 319 N Tarragona St, Pensacola, FL
3pm
$10

We’re doing a matinee this month for our songwriter series, so come to the Handlebar at 3 pm Aug 25th for another great local show, featuring Robbie Paynter from About to Sweat @robbie_ze_paynter , Logan Pilcher @logan_pilcher , and Faithe Franklin @_havefaithe , as well as host Katie Dineen @katiedineensings. Songwriters share original songs “in the round” to a listening room. It’ll be a relaxing, inspiring way to spend your Sunday afternoon, indoors in the AC, and with time to enjoy your evening afterwards. Tickets will be $10 at the door, or online at the Handlebar website for advance sales.
