Alvvays, Silversun Pickups, Beach Fossils, Drug Church

alvvayssilversun pickupsbeach fossilsdrug church
Community Maritime Park

We’re super excited to welcome Drug Church in November. Their new album Prude just came out a week ago and we’re stoked to see their new songs live. _ For over a decade, Drug Church have been building a very strong case that they’re the best loud guitar band in the game; their fifth full-length PRUDE–a 28-minute blast of aggression, melody, irreverence, and genuine heart–feels like the undeniable proof. The album is so downright satisfying it tricks you into thinking there’s nothing all that surprising about a difficult-to-pigeonhole punk band from Albany, NY, with a name like Drug Church somehow having a career at all, much less one that would last over 10 years and qualify them as the best band going. But before you start trying to think of who might have them beat (good luck), consider what just might be the key to Drug Church’s unexpected staying power: Don’t take it too seriously.
