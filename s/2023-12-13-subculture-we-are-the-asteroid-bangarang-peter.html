We Are The Asteroid, Bangarang Peter, Taught Behavior, Dead Devils, Crux

We are incredibly stoked to announce that we will be hosting @wearetheasteroidofficial on December 13th as they make their way into Florida during their tour! Consisting of members from the bands Butthole Surfers / Pain Teens / Ed Hall, they have toured the United States and Japan a few times with Flipper (featuring David Yow) and last year toured for 2 months supporting The Melvins! We are super excited to bring them to Pensacola and provide them proper support with local heavy weights @bangarangpeterband @taughtbehavior @aternaldeaddevils and @cruxtheband Don't miss this amazing opportunity to catch this show!
